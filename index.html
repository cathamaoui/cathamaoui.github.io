How It Works:

Pending ANBLPN and NANB approval. Medloc is where nurses can post their availability and be matched with potential employment. It works around you not the other way around.

  • Step 1. Create Your Profile: Sign up in minutes and build your professional profile.
  • Step 2. Get Verified: Upload your required documents for verification.
  • Step 3. Set Your Availability: Update your calendar to show when you're available.
  • Step 4. Receive Shift Offers: Get notified about open shifts that match your profile.
